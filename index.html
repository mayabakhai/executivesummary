Investment thesis

01

The Rational Consumer: AI gives consumers the leverage that used to belong to enterprises. We back the products that make comparing, buying, scheduling, and deciding feel native in this new behavior.

02

Return to the Physical: As digital creation gets cheaper, digital attention and software wrappers get devalued. The next edge is in physical products, real-world operations, health, food, housing, infrastructure, and other scarce assets made more powerful by software.

03

We Fund the Net New: We like ideas with no obvious comp. If consensus capital can underwrite it on a spreadsheet, it is probably already late. Spice is built to invest before the market has the language.
